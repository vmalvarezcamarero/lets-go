Test report example
====================

---> listSpecies endpoint

* TEST 1:

* Input:

http://127.0.0.1:8080/listSpecies?limit=3

* output:

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
<head>
    <meta charset="UTF-8">
    <title>LIST OF SPECIES IN THE BROWSER</title>
</head>
<body style="background-color: lightgrey;">
<body>
   The total number of species in the ensembl is : 310 <br>
   The limit you have selected is : 3 <br>
   The names of the species are : <ul><li>little spotted kiwi</li><li>Indian glassy fish</li><li>blue tit</li>
</body>
</html>

* TEST 2:

* Input:

http://127.0.0.1:8080/listSpecies?limit=3444

* output:

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
<head>
    <meta charset="UTF-8">
    <title>LIST OF SPECIES IN THE BROWSER</title>
</head>
<body style="background-color: lightgrey;">
<body>
    <p>Number of sequences found: 310</p>
    <p>The limit selected: 3444</p>
    <p></p>
        <p>Name of each specie: </p>
        <ul><li>little spotted kiwi</li><li>Indian glassy fish</li><li>blue tit</li>... ("It would end with the specie number 310")

* Input:

http://127.0.0.1:8080/listSpecies?limit=a
